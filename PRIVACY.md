# Privacy Policy

**AgentRune** — Last updated: March 2026

## Overview

AgentRune is a mobile remote control for AI coding agents. It connects your phone to a server running on your own computer over your local network.

## Data Collection

AgentRune does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

- **No analytics** — No usage tracking, no telemetry
- **No accounts required** — The app works without creating an account
- **No cloud storage** — All data stays on your local network between your phone and computer

## Local Network Communication

AgentRune communicates between your phone and your computer over your local WiFi network (LAN). This traffic does not leave your local network unless you explicitly configure it otherwise.

## Optional Services

- **AgentLore Account** — If you choose to log in with AgentLore, your device ID and authentication token are stored locally on your phone. AgentLore may store your device registration for cloud relay features.
- **Donations/Subscriptions** — Processed by Lemon Squeezy. Their privacy policy applies: https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy

## Camera Permission

The app requests camera access solely for scanning QR codes during the pairing process. No images are stored or transmitted.

## Data Storage

All session data, terminal output, and configuration are stored locally on your computer (the server) and your phone (the client). Nothing is sent to any third-party server.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted in this file and in the app's store listing.
